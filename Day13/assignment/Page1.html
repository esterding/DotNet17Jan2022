Welcome To Clinic Management System

About the project

A clinic management system with two users

  • Patient
  • Doctor

Functionality of Patient's Portal

Patient able to :

  • make appointment
  • amend patient's upcoming appointment
  • check on patient's appointment
  • make payment

Functionality of Doctor's Portal

Doctor able to :

  • check on upcoming appointment
  • check on previous appointment
  • raise fees and add report on past appointment
